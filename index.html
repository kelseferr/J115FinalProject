Meet Kelsey Ferrell

rockstar, comedian, memer, author, baker

About

Kelsey Ferrell is an award winning performer based out of Santa Cruz/Berkeley. Whether performing stand up comedy or music under her artist name, Feral, Ferrell is known for her honesty and vulnerability onstage. Ferrell also runs a meme page online, @kelseferr where she creates original content. Ferrell likes to read memoirs and bake treats in her spare time.

Biography

Kelsey Ferrell was born on July 22nd, 1998 in Santa Cruz, California. After a successful debut at Valencia Elementary Shcool, Ferrell made a brief appearance at Aptos Junior High before going on to Pacific Collegiate School, where she graduated in 2016. She chose to continue her education at UC Berkeley, where she majored in Development Studies and minored in both Global Poverty and Practice and Journalism. During her time at Berkeley, she was a active member of Songwriting at Berkeley and a writer and eventually editor in chief of satire publication The Free Peach. In October 2018, Ferrell released her first album, Trauma Portfolio, which is available on Spotify and all other major streaming services. Ferrell also studied abroad at the University of Sussex in Brighton, England in Spring 2019. While studying at University of Sussex, she joined their Comedy Society and began performing stand up comedy. She had performed in multiple states in the U.S. and internationally. Ferrell is set to graduate UC Berkeley in August 2020. After college, she has plans to join Americorps NCCC, where she will be stationed in Aurora, Colorado, serving the southwest U.S.

In the Words of Her Best Friend

Ferrell's best friend, Liam Coyle says that if he had to describe Ferrell to someone who has never met her, he would say: "Okay, she's great, but she's a lot, so just give her a minute."

Coyle stated that he streams Ferrell's music regularly, saying "it's alwasy impressive to me the variety between a song like Guest House and Soup." He says her lyrics are "raw and surprsingly melodic."

"She's a very talented lyricist with a good ear for what she wants her songs to sound like," continued Coyle. He said his favorite song of Ferrell's was Lonely Planet.

Coyle is also familiar with Ferrell's stand up comedy routines, from watching videos of her sets. "It's like her music: brash, honest, and deeply personal," he describes. "A good artist I think knows their subject pretty well and Kelsey knows herself pretty damn well," Coyle concluded.

Personal Interests

Aside from music and stand up comedy, Ferrell enjoys reading (especially memoirs), maintaining her meme page, writing in her journal, baking treats (she's an alum of UC Berkeley's famous baking decal), and shopping for vintage 90s and y2k fashion. Her wardrobe is of great personal pride to her, as is her bookshelf. Ferrell likes listening to the podcast My Favorite Murder, and The Mortified Podcast. Ferrell also likes going to see live comedy and live music. Her favorite way to travel is via roadtrip. She also is a vegetarian and enjoys vegetarian cooking. You can find her currenly working as a shift lead at the Penny Ice Creamery in Aptos, California. But in October 2020, she will be moving to Aurora, Colorado to serve in Americorps NCCC in the Southwest region.

Coming Soon

Ferrell has enough songs written for a second album and hopes to get to the recording studio once the pandemic is over. She also hopes to get back onstage as a comedian once live shows are active again. Lastly, she hopes to produce a comedic mini series on her youtube channel in the next few years.
